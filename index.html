Model caveats

  • The optimizer maximizes summed EV, not risk-adjusted utility.
  • It does not model positive stack correlation.
  • It can overvalue defensive-mid form (appearance/CS-driven points).
  • One knockout match is high variance. Floor and role security matter.
  • Fixture priors are hand-set; EV is only as good as those inputs.
