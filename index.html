How the model works

Player EVappearance floor + form-above-baseline × fixture mult, start-adjusted, then scaled to realized scoring. Summed form overstates a real round, so a calibration factor pulls projections to the level league rounds actually reach (41 to 109 so far).
Differential EVEV × (1 − ownership). Rank leverage, not raw points.
Scout EV+2 expected for sub-5% ownership producers.
Optimizermaximises summed EV under budget, 2/5/5/3, 3-per-nation.
Judgment layeroverrides for floor, ceiling, correlation, rotation, knockout variance.

Model caveats

  • The optimizer maximizes summed EV, not risk-adjusted utility.
  • It does not model positive stack correlation.
  • It can overvalue defensive-mid form (appearance/CS-driven points).
  • One knockout match is high variance. Floor and role security matter.
  • Fixture priors are hand-set; EV is only as good as those inputs.
